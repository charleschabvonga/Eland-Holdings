ABOUT US

Eland Group is a fast growing sales and distribution company with a wide range of products and services that are used in mining & industry and have several hardware and wholesale & Groceries outlets in Zimbabwe.

Our products & services cater for individuals, institutions, corporate or any other clientele in need of our various products & services. We also offer general procurement, international transportation logistics solutions either by road, air or sea freight and clearing and forwarding of goods.

Vision

To be the sole distributor and supplier of reputable brands in mining & industry, hardware and wholesale & groceries on the continent.

Mission

To consistently satisfy our customer requirements through superior service delivery by providing innovative and reliable technical solutions that are sustainable and affordable.

Values

Professionalism
Target Oriented
Teamwork
Quality Assurance

Our Products & Services

In order to meet the demand of our valued clients we have a wide range of services which include special advisory services on which products to choose following the latest market trends and quality choices to bring the best results at affordable rates.
